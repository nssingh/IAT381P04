Synopsis

A new threat in town has caused the business to be disrupted for all dealers. Crews are being wiped out and no one knows who's causing it, except for the name Lazlo. Two thugs are making their routine trip, but things begin to go wrong. Their trust begins to break, as fear settles in and they realize that Lazlo is after them too.

My Role

I was tasked with creating an interactive narrative, creating the script, hiring actors, filming and producing a short interactive film. The project timeline was only 10 days for completion.

Individual Project

Tasks: Director, Editor, Screenwriter

Tools: DSLR, Light Kit, Audio Equipment, Final Cut X, Adobe Audition

Outcome

Received an A+ for creating a compelling narrative and having a high quality of filming, editing and presentation. The ending was highly enjoyed by audience and known through their feedback.

Shows ability to create unique narrative with multiple paths for user to take that appeals to a wide ranged audience. Ability to create, film and edit video with specific requirements and time constraints to a high standard.
