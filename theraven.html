Summary

JOHN IS MARRIED TO HIS BEAUTIFUL WIFE LENORE. THEY HAVE A HAPPY RELATIONSHIP UNTIL JOHN CHEATS ON LENORE WITH THE MYSTERIOUS RAVEN. THIS RESULTS WITH LENORE COMMITTING SUICIDE. JOHN IS HAUNTED BY WHAT HE HAS DONE TO LENORE, BUT THIS DOES NOT STOP HIS RELATIONSHIP WITH THE RAVEN. HE BEGINS TO LOSE HIS MIND, BEING HAUNTED BY THE MEMORY OF THE LATE LENORE.

I was tasked with creating a short narrative, creating the script, hiring professional actors, filming and producing a short film.

Tasks: Director, Editor, Screenwriter

Tools: DSLR, Light Kit, Audio Equipment, Final Cut X, Adobe Audition
